ABOUT

本公司成立于2007年,我們的開發與企劃團隊用心于結合國際最新的流行元素與臺灣專業工廠的優勢,創造出創新流行且高附加價值的面料。 產品系列伴隨著不同多元素材以及最流行的顏色,有緹花、刺繡、蕾絲、印花及複合性工藝等等設計面料。我們服務宗旨: 製造最時尚創新的面料,為客戶創造更多價值。

Jing Wei textile Co., Ltd was founded in 2007, our R&D team co-operates with Taiwanese reputable mills and focuses on producing highly stylish and value added products along with the latest fashion element for the international market. Our products covers a variety of materials and a fashionable colour range including jacquard, embroidery, lace, print and multi-material textile. Our mission: To produce highly fashionable and advanced textiles in order to create value for our clients.
